Ideating with Research and Storyboards

With our problem in mind, we researched current articles to determine whether our problem was indeed relevant and discover what the key factors may be that we could tackle with our solution.

Research Discoveries

Here are some of the key facts we discovered during our research.

Drivers spend about 17 hours a year looking for parking
About $345 of fuel, emissions, and time is wasted per year looking for parking
2 out of 3 drivers feel stressed when finding parking

Storyboards

After doing some research, we had a better idea of what was paining people in their parking experiences. With a clearer idea of the problem in mind, we sketched out a few user storyboards to illustrate our problem and possible solutions.

Transportation: Improving How We Move

The Parking Problem

San Diego's downtown, like many flourishing cities, has a growing traffic problem. Several studies show that poor parking systems are a major factor.

Spot, our solution to the parking problem, a mobile parking app,was designed and entered for the 2017 D4SD Challenge.
